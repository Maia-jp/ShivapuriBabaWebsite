# Shivapuri Baba — Books, Media & References

*Where to find them, organized by source type. Prices and stock change constantly and several titles are out of print, so links point to vendors/catalogues rather than fixed prices. Internet Archive links are free controlled-lending (borrow), not purchase.*

---

## 1. First-hand books (by people who met him)

### Long Pilgrimage: The Life and Teaching of the Shivapuri Baba
J. G. Bennett with Thakur Lal Manandhar (1965). *The primary biography — start here.* In print.
- Bennett Books (publisher): https://bennettbooks.org/product/long-pilgrimage/
- Vajra Books, Kathmandu: https://vajrabookshop.com/product/long-pilgrimage-the-life-and-teachings-of-the-shivapuri-baba/
- Pilgrims Book House, Kathmandu: https://pilgrimsbooks.com (search the title)
- The Quest, Kathmandu (also arranges supply in Banaras/India): https://thequest.org.np/products
- Amazon (2016 reprint, ISBN 978-1530624317): https://www.amazon.com/Long-Pilgrimage-Life-Teaching-Shivapuri/dp/1530624312
- AbeBooks (new & used): https://www.abebooks.com/9781530624317/Long-Pilgrimage-Life-Teaching-Shivapuri-1530624312/plp
- Exotic India (ships from India): https://www.exoticindiaart.com/book/details/long-pilgrimage-life-and-teaching-of-shivapuri-baba-naw319/
- Borrow/read free: https://archive.org/details/longpilgrimageli0000jgbe

### Witness: The Story of a Search
J. G. Bennett (autobiography, 1962). *Contains his own first-hand account of the 1961–62 visits.* In print (modern editions).
- Bennett Books: https://bennettbooks.org/product/witness-the-story-of-a-search/
- Amazon (Bennett Books ed.): https://www.amazon.com/Witness-Search-Bennett-Spiritual-Classic/dp/1881408027
- AbeBooks (incl. 1962 Hodder & Stoughton first editions): https://www.abebooks.com/book-search/title/witness/author/bennett-john/

### The Shivapuri Baba and His Message: Four Lectures on a Great Indian Sage
J. G. Bennett (lectures delivered London, 1962; Collected Works vol. 11). *Short, accessible summary of the teaching.* In print.
- Amazon (ISBN 978-1533489319): https://www.amazon.com/Shivapuri-Baba-His-Message-Collected/dp/1533489319
- eBay (new copies): https://www.ebay.com/itm/388678424421

### Strange Places, Simple Truths
Dr. Ainslie Meares (1969). *The independent second eyewitness — the "Nepal" chapter.* Out of print → secondhand only.
- AbeBooks (used): https://www.abebooks.com/book-search/author/meares-dr-ainslie/book/
- Borrow/read free: https://archive.org/details/strangeplacessim00mearrich

### Right Life: Teachings of the Shivapuri Baba
Renu Lal Singh (Kathmandu, 1975; rev. ed. Coombe Springs Press, 1984). *Disciple's record of his conversations.* Out of print → secondhand / Kathmandu shops.
- Info & editions: https://www.goodreads.com/book/show/62800685-right-life
- Search used copies: AbeBooks and Amazon Marketplace (search "Right Life Shivapuri Baba Renu Lal Singh"); also Vajra Books and Pilgrims Book House in Kathmandu.

### Right Living: The Teaching of Sri Shivapuri Baba
Dr. Y. B. (Yogendra Bhakta) Shrestha Malla. *Disciple compilation; English.* Available mainly from Nepal.
- Shop Ratna Online (Ratna Pustak Bhandar): https://shopratnaonline.com/right-living-the-teachings-of-sri-shivapuri-baba-yb-shrestha-malla/
- Daraz Nepal: https://www.daraz.com.np/products/right-living-the-teaching-of-sri-shivapuri-baba-2410-i128524533.html
- Borrow/read free: https://archive.org/details/right-living.-the-teaching-of-sri-shivapuri-baba-by-dr.-y.-b.-shresta-malla-z-lib.org

### Swadharma
Bishnu Prasad Timilsina (in Nepali). *Disciple compilation.* Hardest to source outside Nepal.
- Try Ratna Pustak Bhandar / Daraz Nepal (search "Swadharma Shivapuri Baba") and Kathmandu bookshops.

## 2. Audio & video

- **The Shivapuri Baba Interviews (1962 audio).** Bennett's own recordings of the Baba, made in Kathmandu on 1–2 April 1962, with Marjorie von Harten and Melissa Marston present. The closest thing to hearing him directly. J. G. Bennett Foundation: https://www.jgbennett.org/product/shivapuri-baba-interviews/
- **Footage of the Shivapuri Baba.** A short film clip held by the J. G. Bennett Foundation — see the Shivapuri Baba archive/tag pages: https://www.jgbennett.org/tag/shivapuri-baba/
- **Supreme Master Television — two-part program** on the Baba (free streaming; devotional framing): Part 1 https://suprememastertv.com/en1/v/238669118313.html · Part 2 https://suprememastertv.com/en1/v/239369362510.html
- **Shivapuri Baba Ashram / Samadhi (YouTube).** A walk-through of the retreat near Pashupatinath: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=nVcAdHnr6EI

## 4. In person

- **Shivapuri Baba Samadhi & Ashram**, Dhrubasthali (Dhruvasthali), in the forest behind the Pashupatinath temple, Kathmandu — his hermitage of the final decades and the site of his shrine; still a meditation/pilgrimage spot.

---

### Quick guidance
- **If you read only one book:** *Long Pilgrimage* (in print, widely available).
- **For the independent outside view:** Meares's *Strange Places, Simple Truths* — secondhand, or borrow free on Internet Archive.
- **To hear him:** the 1962 audio at the J. G. Bennett Foundation.
- **A note on sourcing:** pirated full-text PDFs of several titles circulate online; the Internet Archive links above are the legitimate free-borrowing route, and the publisher/Kathmandu links support the people keeping these works in print.
